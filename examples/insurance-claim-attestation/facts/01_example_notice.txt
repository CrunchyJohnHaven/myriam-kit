FACT 01 — EXAMPLE NOTICE

This publication is an EXAMPLE demonstration of how an insurance
adjuster of record — auto, health, property & casualty, life, or
specialty lines — can use myriam-kit to seal a claim-decision
attestation at the moment a coverage decision is rendered, so that
post-facto revision of the claim record (by either side) becomes
cryptographically detectable. The carrier, claim number, claimant
identifier, evidence hashes, coverage interpretation, payment
decision, and signing adjuster particulars in subsequent facts are
ALL FICTIONAL.

The purpose: show the SHAPE of a real insurance-claim decision seal.
A real attestation would replace each fact body with the actual
artifacts of the actual claim file at the moment of decision.

This kit is intended to compose with — not replace — the existing
claims-handling, state-insurance-department, ERISA, McCarran-Ferguson,
and unfair-claims-settlement-practices regulatory frameworks of the
jurisdiction in which the claim is being adjudicated. The MYRIAM seal
provides Bitcoin-anchored tamper-evidence on top of whatever claim
file, EDI transaction record, ACORD form, or carrier claims-management
system the adjuster already maintains. It does NOT itself decide
coverage, satisfy any state's bad-faith-claims-handling statute, or
substitute for the carrier's regulatory filings.

This fact asserts only that the publication is an example, not a real
insurance-claim decision. No real claim is hereby adjudicated. No real
insured, claimant, third-party claimant, or witness is hereby
identified.
