This page is a sealed publication of 8 facts that together constitute a complete trial pre-registration: trial identification, hypothesis, study design, endpoints, statistical analysis plan, subgroup analyses and exclusion criteria, and pre-registration commitments. The 8 facts have been hashed (SHA-256), combined into a Bitcoin-style Merkle root, and committed to the Bitcoin blockchain via OpenTimestamps against two independent calendar operators.

This kit is a complement to existing trial registries (ClinicalTrials.gov, EU CTR, ISRCTN), not a replacement. A real trial should be registered in the jurisdictional registry AND additionally sealed under MYRIAM.
