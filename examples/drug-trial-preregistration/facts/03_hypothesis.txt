FACT 03 — HYPOTHESIS (FICTIONAL)

PRIMARY HYPOTHESIS

  Treatment with FICT-X-7 at 50 mg orally once daily for 12 weeks will
  produce a statistically significant reduction in the primary endpoint
  (defined in FACT 05) compared to placebo, at the alpha = 0.05 level,
  in the modified intent-to-treat population.

NULL HYPOTHESIS (formally stated for clarity)

  H0: μ_treatment − μ_placebo = 0 on the primary endpoint
  H1: μ_treatment − μ_placebo ≠ 0 on the primary endpoint

EFFECT SIZE OF INTEREST

  The trial is powered to detect a between-group difference of 0.4
  standardized units on the primary endpoint, which the sponsor
  considers the minimum clinically meaningful difference.

This fact asserts only that the above is the trial's pre-registered
hypothesis. It does not assert that the hypothesis is true, that the
effect size is achievable, or that the chosen primary endpoint
captures clinical meaningfulness.
