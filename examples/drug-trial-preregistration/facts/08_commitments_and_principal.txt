FACT 08 — PRE-REGISTRATION COMMITMENTS + SIGNING PRINCIPAL (FICTIONAL)

In the spirit of the MYRIAM protocol's "minimize preventable deaths
over time" objective function, the fictional sponsor commits — in
advance of enrollment — to the following:

  1. PUBLICATION OF RESULTS REGARDLESS OF DIRECTION
     The sponsor commits to publishing the trial results (positive,
     negative, or null) within 12 months of database lock, in a
     peer-reviewed journal or, failing that, on a public archive.
     Suppression of negative or null results would be detectable as
     a deviation from this commitment.

  2. NO POST-HOC ENDPOINT SUBSTITUTION
     If the primary endpoint (FACT 05) fails to show significance,
     the sponsor commits NOT to promote any secondary or exploratory
     endpoint to "primary" in the published report. Secondary
     endpoints will be reported as secondary; exploratory endpoints
     will be reported as exploratory.

  3. NO POST-HOC SUBGROUP MINING PRESENTED AS CONFIRMATORY
     Any subgroup analysis not in FACT 07 must be labeled "post-hoc
     and exploratory" in the published report. The pre-registered
     subgroups in FACT 07 are the only ones that can be claimed as
     pre-specified.

  4. NO STATISTICAL-PLAN DEVIATION WITHOUT SEALED AMENDMENT
     If unforeseen events require deviation from the statistical
     analysis plan in FACT 06 (e.g., enrollment lower than expected,
     analysis error discovered), the deviation will be documented in
     a NEW sealed MYRIAM publication that references this one by
     Merkle root, BEFORE the primary analysis is conducted (and
     therefore before unblinding).

  5. DATA AVAILABILITY
     Within 12 months of publication, the sponsor commits to making
     the de-identified individual-participant dataset available to
     qualified researchers via a controlled-access mechanism. Hashes
     of the canonical dataset will be sealed in a separate MYRIAM
     publication so that the dataset's integrity can be verified.

  6. ADVERSE-EVENT TRANSPARENCY
     All serious adverse events will be reported regardless of
     attribution. The trial's safety database will be made available
     to regulators within statutorily required windows; in addition,
     the sponsor commits to making aggregate safety data publicly
     available within 6 months of database lock.

  7. NO RETROACTIVE CHANGES TO THIS PRE-REGISTRATION
     The sponsor will not silently amend this pre-registration. Any
     necessary amendments will be NEW sealed publications referencing
     this one, with each amendment timestamped via OpenTimestamps.
     Silent amendment is structurally impossible under the seal.

This fact asserts only the commitments and signing principals as
declared for the fictional trial. It does not assert that the
commitments will be honored in practice, or that honoring them is
sufficient for ethical trial conduct.

The commitments are intended as a *forensic anchor*. Any future
deviation from any commitment by the sponsor will be publicly
comparable against this sealed declaration, providing evidence for
regulatory, patient-advocacy, and journalistic accountability.
