FACT 04 — STUDY DESIGN (FICTIONAL)

DESIGN

  Type:                     Randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled,
                            parallel-group
  Allocation ratio:         1:1 (treatment : placebo)
  Randomization:            Centralized, computer-generated, stratified
                            by [fictional stratification variable]
  Blinding:                 Participants, investigators, outcome
                            assessors, and statisticians all blinded
                            until database lock

POPULATION

  Total planned enrollment: 480 participants
  Inclusion criteria:       Adults age 18-70 with [fictional condition Y]
                            of moderate severity (defined by [fictional
                            severity scale] score 12-30 at screening),
                            stable on baseline therapy for ≥3 months
  Exclusion criteria:       (full list pre-registered separately in
                            FACT 07; abbreviated here)
                            - Pregnancy or planned pregnancy
                            - Active or recent (past 12 months)
                              participation in another investigational
                              drug trial
                            - Severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh C)
                            - Known hypersensitivity to FICT-X-7 or
                              excipients

INTERVENTION

  Treatment arm:    FICT-X-7 50 mg orally once daily for 12 weeks
  Placebo arm:      Matching capsule once daily for 12 weeks
  Duration:         12 weeks active treatment; 12 weeks follow-up
                    (24 weeks total per participant)
  Concomitant medications: All baseline therapies continued unchanged;
                    no new therapies for [fictional condition Y] permitted
                    during the trial except for documented rescue

ANALYSIS POPULATIONS (pre-defined)

  - mITT (modified intent-to-treat): all randomized participants who
    received ≥1 dose and have ≥1 post-baseline assessment.
    PRIMARY ANALYSIS POPULATION.
  - Per-protocol: mITT participants who completed ≥8 weeks of
    treatment and had no major protocol deviations.
    SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS POPULATION.
  - Safety: all randomized participants who received ≥1 dose.
    SAFETY ANALYSIS POPULATION.

This fact asserts only the study design as pre-registered for the
fictional trial. It does not assert that the design is appropriate
for the (fictional) condition or that the parameters chosen are
optimal. Design choices are open to peer-review critique; the seal
only proves that the design was committed to *prior* to seeing the
data.
