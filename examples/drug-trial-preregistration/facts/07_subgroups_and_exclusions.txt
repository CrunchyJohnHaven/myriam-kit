FACT 07 — PRE-REGISTERED SUBGROUP ANALYSES + FULL EXCLUSION CRITERIA (FICTIONAL)

PRE-REGISTERED SUBGROUP ANALYSES

  Subgroup analyses below are PRE-SPECIFIED. They will be reported
  regardless of direction or significance. They are EXPLORATORY (do not
  affect the primary inference).

  S1. Sex (male / female / non-binary or unspecified)
  S2. Age group (18-44 / 45-64 / 65-70)
  S3. Baseline FSSS severity (mild 12-17 / moderate 18-24 / severe 25-30)
  S4. Region (North America / non-North America — N/A for this trial,
      noted for cross-trial comparability)
  S5. Concomitant baseline therapy (yes / no)

  Each subgroup analysis uses the same ANCOVA model as the primary
  analysis, with treatment-by-subgroup interaction tested at
  alpha = 0.10 (exploratory only). Forest plots will be reported.

ANY POST-HOC SUBGROUP IDENTIFIED AFTER UNBLINDING IS NOT IN THIS LIST.
Post-hoc subgroups, if reported, must be labeled as such in the trial
report and cannot be claimed as confirmatory.

FULL EXCLUSION CRITERIA (pre-registered)

  E1.  Age < 18 or > 70 at screening
  E2.  Pregnancy, breastfeeding, or planning pregnancy during the trial
  E3.  Inability to provide informed consent
  E4.  Severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh class C) at screening
  E5.  eGFR < 30 mL/min/1.73m² at screening
  E6.  Known hypersensitivity to FICT-X-7 or any excipient
  E7.  Active or recent (past 12 months) participation in another
       investigational drug trial
  E8.  Cancer treatment within past 5 years (excluding non-melanoma
       skin cancer and stable cervical CIS)
  E9.  Uncontrolled cardiovascular disease (NYHA class III/IV; MI or
       stroke within past 6 months)
  E10. Active substance use disorder (excluding tobacco, caffeine) within
       past 12 months
  E11. Use of [fictional class of contraindicated medications] within
       past 30 days
  E12. Inability to comply with the trial schedule (per investigator
       assessment)

This fact asserts only the pre-registered subgroup analyses and the
full exclusion criteria. It does not assert that the subgroup choices
are exhaustive of meaningful subpopulations, or that the exclusion
criteria are appropriate for all clinical contexts.

CRITICAL NOTE: addition of new exclusion criteria after this sealing,
or post-hoc subgroup analyses presented as confirmatory, will be
DETECTABLE as deviations from this sealed pre-registration.
