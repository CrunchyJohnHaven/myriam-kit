FACT 06 — SIGNING PRINCIPAL (FICTIONAL)

  Sponsor:              Fictional BioPharma Inc. (FICTIONAL)
  Primary signatory:    Dr. [Fictional Name], QA Director
  Secondary signatory:  Dr. [Fictional Name], Head of Manufacturing
  Date sealed:          2026-04-26
  Cryptographic key:    (none in this example; a real attestation would
                        attach a PGP fingerprint for the QA director,
                        so future statements claimed to be from QA can
                        be cryptographically verified)

In a real attestation, the signing principal section should include:

  - The QA-release-authorized individual's legal name and license number
    (where required by jurisdiction)
  - The manufacturer's regulatory establishment ID (FDA / EU / WHO PQ)
  - A PGP public key fingerprint OR equivalent (so future amendments,
    recall notices, or stability updates can be cryptographically tied
    to this same principal)
  - A canonical contact channel for regulators and recall coordination

The signing principal SHOULD NOT include in this fact:

  - Personal phone numbers or home addresses of QA staff
  - Trade secrets about the manufacturing process (these belong in
    confidential regulatory submissions, not public seals)
  - Detailed customer lists or distribution destinations beyond
    high-level regional summary

This fact asserts only the signing principal identification fields for
the fictional batch release as declared. It does not assert any of
those fields about any real manufacturer or individual.
