This fact is the LOAD-BEARING quantitative content of the seal. Each
integer above is what the project developer and verifier jointly attest
at the moment of attestation (FACT 02 + FACT 05 timestamps). Any later
silent revision to any of these integers would produce a different

and would fail verification against the Bitcoin-committed root.

This fact asserts only the per-vintage emissions / removals
quantification as computed under the cited methodology at the moment
of attestation. It does not assert that the methodology correctly
models the underlying climate phenomenon, that the baseline counter-
factual is realistic, that the buffer-pool reserve is sufficient to
cover real non-permanence risk, or that any tonne represents a real
climate benefit.
