FACT 01 — EXAMPLE NOTICE

This publication is an EXAMPLE demonstration of how a carbon-credit
project developer, third-party verifier, or registry can use myriam-kit
to seal a carbon-attestation publication at the moment of issuance,
removal verification, or emissions certification. The project name,
registry ID, location, methodology, sensor readings, verifier identity,
issued serial numbers, and signing principals are ALL FICTIONAL.

The purpose: show the SHAPE of a real carbon attestation seal. A real
attestation would replace each fact body with the actual elements of
the actual project and verification cycle.

This kit is intended to compose with — not replace — existing carbon
infrastructure (Verra/VCS, Gold Standard, Climate Action Reserve,
American Carbon Registry, Article 6.4 mechanism, CDP, EU ETS MRV
processes, ISO 14064/14065 verification). The MYRIAM seal provides
Bitcoin-anchored tamper-evidence on top of whatever registry workflow
the project already follows.

Carbon credits are politically and scientifically contested. This kit
makes no claim about whether any sealed credit represents real,
additional, permanent, or non-leakage-prone climate benefit. The seal
records what was attested at a moment in time, in the exact byte form
it was attested. Nothing more.

This fact asserts only that the publication is an example, not a real
carbon attestation. No real carbon credit is hereby issued, no real
removal is hereby verified, and no real emissions claim is hereby
certified.
