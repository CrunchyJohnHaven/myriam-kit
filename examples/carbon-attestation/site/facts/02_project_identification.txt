This fact binds the entire sealed publication to a named, traceable
project on a named registry. Anchoring the project identifier and the
methodology hash at attestation time prevents post-hoc claims that
"the methodology was always different" or "the project boundary was
always different."

This fact asserts only the identity of the project, its registry
record, methodology citation + hash, geographic scope, vintage, and
co-benefits claim status at attestation time. It does not assert that
the project is additional (would not have happened without carbon
finance), permanent (carbon will remain sequestered over the relevant
time horizon), free of leakage (emissions are not displaced rather
than avoided), or that the methodology itself is scientifically valid.
