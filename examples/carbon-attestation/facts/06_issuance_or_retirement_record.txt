FACT 06 — ISSUANCE OR RETIREMENT RECORD (FICTIONAL)

This fact records the credit-lifecycle state on the registry of record
(FACT 02) at the moment of attestation. The kit DOES NOT issue or
retire credits — only the registry can. The kit anchors the registry
state so that silent post-attestation changes (re-issuance of cancelled
credits, double-counting across registries, retirement records
disappearing) are publicly detectable.

This fact asserts only the credit-lifecycle state as recorded on the
registry at the moment of attestation. It does not assert that the
registry's recording is correct, that the credits are not also being
counted toward some other claim on some other ledger, that the buffer
pool will compensate for actual future reversals, or that any
retirement beneficiary's net-zero / carbon-neutral claim is supported
by the credits they retired.
