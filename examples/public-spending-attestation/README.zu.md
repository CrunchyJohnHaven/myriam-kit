# Ithulkithi Lokuvala Lobufakazi Bokusebenzisa Imali Yomphakathi

*Uhumusho lwesiZulu — ngenguqulo yokuqala yesiNgisi, bheka [README.md](README.md)*

Isibonelo se-myriam-kit esisebenzayo ngokuphelele sohulumeni bezakhamuzi, bezifundazwe,
bezifunda, nabezizwe abafuna ukubophezela ngokukhonkretha imibhalo yokwenza
ibhajethi (ukuvunyelwa kwezinkontileka, ukukhokhwa kwezimali, ukwamukelwa
kokuhlinzekwa) ngomzuzu wokukhishwa, ukuze ukushintshwa okuthulile kwemibhalo
yokusebenzisa imali yomphakathi kuthole ukubonakala komphakathi.

Ukuvalwa kuyi-**foreni labanikazi**, hhayi isitifiketi sobuqotho. Kurekhoda
lokho igunya lezezimali elikubikile ngosuku lapho kubikwa khona, ngendlela
engakwazi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi ukuhlolwa ngokuthulile ngaphandle kokukhipha
ukungavumelani okubonakalayo komphakathi okukhonkretha.

---

## Yini le thulkithi engayenzi (qala ngokufunda)

Ukuvalwa kuyi-timestamp yekhonkretha yokuthi yini eyaboniswa. Akukwazi
ukuqinisekisa ukuthi okwaboniswa kwakuyi-**semthethweni, kufanele, noma
kuyiqiniso**. Lezi zindlela zokwehluleka **zingaphandle kwendawo yokuvala**:

1. **Inkohlakalo yangaphezulu kanye nezimali zokufihla.** Uma ukuthengwa kwakulungiswe,
   umthengisi wakhethwa ngenxa yokufunjathiswa, isizathu sokukhetha umthombo owodwa
   sasiqanjwa, noma inkontileka yayikhukhumele ngephesenti elivunyelwene lezimali zokufihla —
   ukuvalwa kubophezela ukuthengiselwana okurekhodelwe ngentengo ekhukhumele
   kanye nomthengisi othandwayo ngobuqotho obukhulu. Ukuvalwa kubuka okuphumayo
   kuhlelo lwezezimali; akunamandla okubona inkohlakalo engaphezulu kokukhishwa.
   I-*Operação Lava Jato* yaseBrazil yakhumbula iziphesenti ezikhokhwayo ezilawulwa
   isigungu seziphesenti ezingaba ngu-3% kuzinkulungwane zezinkontileka zePetrobras
   ezazingavinjwe ngokukhishwa njengezizijwayelekile.

2. **Ukufihlwa kwabanikazi ngokungatholakali kwezinkampani.** Inkontileka
   evunyelwe ku-"Acme Construction Ltd." ivalwe ngegama lebhizinisi
   eluhlwini lomphakathi. Uma "Acme" kuyilo elilawulwa ngumuntu ophambili
   wezombusazwe ongabikwanga, noma kungeneyo izinkampani ezimbalwa
   ezilawulwa yisigungu esisodwa, ukuvalwa kubophezela ibhizinisi
   eliphezulu. Ukuvulwa kwabanikazi yindawo EHLUKILE (FATF Recommendation
   24, EU 5AMLD/6AMLD, US Corporate Transparency Act) okuphumayo kwayo
   kudinga iphrothokholi yayo yokuvala.

3. **Ukukhwabanisa amabhuku angaphezulu kokukhishwa.** Uma igunya lezezimali
   ngokwalo liqamba irekhodi ngaphambi kokuba livalwe — izinkontileka
   ezingekho, abasebenzi abangekho ohlwini lokukhokha, ukwamukelwa
   kokuhlinzekwa okungekho — ukuvalwa kubophezela ukuqamba. Ukuvalwa
   akukwazi ukubona ukuthi irekhodi alilona iqiniso ngomzuzu lapho
   lirekhodwa; kungabona kuphela ukuthi irekhodi lishintshiwe EMUVA
   kokuvalwa.

4. **Ukushicilela okukhethiwe / ukuthatha okuhle.** Igunya lezezimali
   lingashicilela **iqoqo elincane** lokuthengiselwana — lokho okuhlanzekile —
   futhi livale lokho kuphela. Ukuvalwa kubophezela lokho okusekushicilelweni;
   akubopheleli izwe lokuthengiselwana okwakufanele kube khona
   ekushicilelweni. I-FACT 07 yale thulkithi (ukuvulwa okuhambisanayo
   kanye nerekhodi lokwala) yikho ukuvikelwa kwezakhiwo, kodwa kuncike
   ekutheni igunya elikhipha lithembekile mayelana nalokho elikhetha
   ukungakuvali.

5. **Ukusebenzisa imali okungaphandle kwamabhuku noma okungaphandle kwebhajethi.**
   Ukusebenzisa imali komphakathi okudlula ngamabhizinisi ahlanganiswe
   nombuso, izimali zomthamo wobukhosi, izimoto zenhloso ekhethekile,
   noma ama-akhawunti okwethemba angaphandle kwebhajethi kungase
   kungavezi ebhajethi okuvalwa kuyo. Ukuvalwa kurekhoda lokho okwabikwa
   ngaphakathi kwesistim yebhajethi; ukusebenzisa imali ngaphandle
   kwesistim kungaphandle kwendawo yokuvala. I-Iraq Coalition Provisional
   Authority (CPA) ezimpikiswano zika-2003–2004 zazibandakanya cishe
   uSAB$9 billion kumakhokho emali okwarekhodwa kwawo kwakulahlekile
   ngokwesigaba; ukuvala lokho okwakukhona ngomzuzu wokukhokhwa kwakuyobophezela
   kona, kodwa bekungenakudala amarekhodi i-CPA engawagcinanga.

6. **Ingcindezi yokuchaza ngemva kokukhishwa.** Ukweqile okuvalwe, umthombo
   wodwa ovaliwe, noma i-oda lokushintsha elivaliwe ingaqondwa kabusha
   ngokuhamba kwesikhathi nganoma yiluphi uhlaka olufunwa yiziphathimandla
   zezombusazwe. Ukuvalwa kubophezela AMAQINISO ngosuku lokukhishwa,
   kodwa akwahluleli kusengaphambili indaba lawo maqiniso ayozosekela ngokuhamba
   kwesikhathi.

7. **Ubufakazi obuphoqwe.** Uma umphathi wezezimali oqondene aphoqelelwa
   ukuba asayinde ubufakazi obumelele okuthengiselwana okungesilo iqiniso,
   ukuvalwa kubophezela ubufakazi obuphoqwe ngokusayina okuphoqwe. Ukuvalwa
   kuqinisekisa UBANI osayinile (FACT 08), hhayi UKUTHI KUNGANI esayinile.
   Ukuvala ubufakazi obuphoqwe kukhiqiza irekhodi lomphakathi elingaguquki
   lokuphumayo okuphoqiwe — kuyasiza ekuphendulekeni kamuva, kodwa
   akusilo ukuvikelwa ekuphoqweni okumzuzu.

8. **Ukuqhathaniswa kwezizinda.** Indaba evaliwe yokusebenzisa imali
   iyafundeka kuphela ngokumelene nohlelo lomthetho lwezizinda elikhiphayo.
   "Kungani uMasipala X akhokhe u-Y wesevisi Z lapho uMasipala W
   akhokhe kuphela u-V?" yimibuzo yokuhlaziya yokuqhathanisa ngaphansi
   kokuvalwa. Ukulinganiswa kwezizinda kuyitayisi yokuhlaziya kudatha
   evaliwe, hhayi impahla yokuvala.

**Isigaba sokuthembela kwezimo emiqala enkulu ngaphezulu yingxenye eyisisekelo yale thulkithi.**
Iziphathimandla zezezimali kanye nezombusazwe ezikholwa ukuthi ukuvalwa kulingana
nobuqotho zizosebenzisa le thulkithi njengesivikelo. Ithulkithi kufanele isho ngokucacile:
lokhu kuyisiqalo se-*non-repudiation primitive sezikhathi zokukhishwa*, hhayi
ubufakazi bobuqotho bokusebenzisa imali.

## Yini ithulkithi okufanele yenqabele ukuvala

Ithulkithi ilungiswe ukuze yenqabe lezi zinhlobo zamaqiniso ngisho noma
umsebenzisi onelungelo elisemthethweni lokusayinda eziwafake:

- **Amagama omsebenzi wakahulumeni ongumuntu ngamunye angaphansi kwezinga lokusayinda
  okuphambili.** Akukho qiniso elingabiza isisebenzi sehhovisi lokuthenga ongumuntu
  ngamunye, abakwesimali ngabanye, amalungu ehlandla lokukala ngabanye,
  noma umuntu ongowemvelo ongekho endimeni yokuphendulekisa eqondene
  nomphakathi. Ingozi yokujeziswa ngabasebenzi besisebenzi ungaphezu
  kwenzuzo yokuvuleka; ubunikazi babo buvalwe ngaphakathi
  kwemilawulo yangaphakathi (i-FACT 05 hash ye-log yokusayinda), hhayi
  emzimbeni wobufakazi.

- **I-PII yomthengisi.** Akukho qiniso elingafaka izinombolo zama-akhawunti
  ebhange umthengisi, amagama abasebenzi bomthengisi ngabanye, amakheli
  asekhaya omthengisi, izinombolo zentela zomthengisi zabantu bemvelo,
  ukuhlukaniswa kwentengo kwangaphakathi okufakwe isakhiwo sezindleko
  zomthengisi, noma noma yiliphi enye intsika ye-PII yokuhweba okushicilelwa
  kwayo kuhlukumeza izinhloso zomthengisi ngaphandle kokulwa nenkohlakalo.
  Lolu lwazi lungaba HASHED (FACT 05) kodwa lungashicilelwa.

- **Ukusebenzisa imali okuhlanzwe ngokokuphepha.** Ukusebenzisa imali
  okuhlanzwe ngaphansi kwemithetho yokuphepha kwezwe AKUKWAZI
  ukuvalwa ngaphansi kwale thulkithi. Ukuphathwa okuhlanzwe yinkinga
  ehlukile enezidingo ezahlukile zekhonkretha nezomthetho. Ukukhutshwa
  ngokwakho kuvulwe ku-FACT 07 R3 ukuze umfundi azi ukuthi
  ukushicilela kuyingxenye ngokuklamile.

- **Ukuphoqelelwa kwamandla entela ngokumelene nabantu bemvelo abakhonjisiwe.**
  Yize izindleko zokuphoqelelwa kuyizinkokho zomphakathi, ukubiza
  abathela intela ngabanye ngaphansi kokuphoqelelwa kuyaliwe: ukulimaza
  ubunikazi kungaphezu kwenzuzo yokuvuleka, futhi ukuvuleka okufanayo
  kukhona ngaphansi kokubikwa kwenkantolo yentela.

- **Ukuthengiselwana okwangaphambi kokukhishwa.** Akukho nkontileka
  ngaphambi kokunikezwa okusemthethweni; akukho khokhwa ngaphambi
  kokukhishwa okusemthethweni. Ukuvala inkontileka olungukuhlinzeka noma
  inkokho elindile njengokuthi yenziwe kakhiqiza ukuqiniseka okungelona iqiniso.

- **Ukuvalwa ngomuntu ngaphandle komphathi wezezimali oqondene.** Inkampani,
  umthengisi, ilungu lekomidi, umbiki wezindaba, noma umbukeli
  akakwazi ukuvala "irekhodi lokusebenzisa imali yomphakathi" esikhundleni
  segunya lezezimali elalikhipha. (Bangase bavale ukubuka kwabo
  ngaphansi kwabaphathi babo, kushicilelo elihlukile.)

- **Ukuqondisa "okukhululekile enkohlakalweni."** Ithulkithi yenqaba
  ukuvala noma yiluphi uhlaka lwesimo se-"lokhu kusebenzisa imali kwakusemthethweni
  futhi kukhululekile enkohlakalweni" noma "ukuthenga kwakufakazelwe
  kufanele." Lezi zimangalo zingaphezu kwendawo yokwazi ye-thulkithi.

- **Ukushicilela okushiya phandle ukuvulwa okuhambisanayo kweFACT 07.** Ukushicilela
  okuthengiselwana ngaphandle kwerekhodi lokuvuleka kanye nokwala elihambisanayo
  akusiyo ukushicilela okusemthethweni ko-MYRIAM ngaphansi kwale thulkithi.

Ukwala ngokwakho kungaba ngevaliwe njengeqiniso ("Leli qembu licele
ukuvalwa kwa-X; ithulkithi yenqaba ngokomthetho-wokwala u-Y"), idala irekhodi
lomphakathi lokuzama ukusetshenziswa kabi. Endaweni lapho ingcindezi yezombusazwe
ezisebenzini zezezimali ibhalwe, irekhodi lezicelo **ezenqatshelwe**
yingxenye yokufakazela ubuqotho.

## Yini ithulkithi EYIVALAYO

Amaqiniso ayisi-8 ngomzuzu wobufakazi bokwenza ibhajethi:

1. **Isaziso sesibonelo** — sithi ukushicilela kuyisibonelo (ubufakazi bangempela
   bukhipha noma bubuyisela lokhu).
2. **Isizinda nesisekelo somthetho** — yiliphi ibhizinisi likahulumeni
   elikhiphayo, ngaphansi kwaliphi igunya lomthetho, ngesiphi isikhathi
   sezezimali.
3. **Inkokhelo noma isithombe sebhajethi** — imigqa eqondile ebhajethini
   evunyiwe esebenza, kanye nama-hash ezinto zebhajethi nanoma yiziphi
   izichibiyelo / ukudluliselwa / ukuhlelwa kabusha okusetshenziswe.
4. **Manifesto yenkontileka noma yokukhokhwa** — irekhodi nge-transaction:
   inani, igama lomthengisi onomthetho (akukho PII yomuntu ngamunye),
   inhloso, idethi, uhlobo lokuthenga, isisekelo sokuvunyelwa, i-hash
   yedokhumenti yenkontileka.
5. **Ama-hash amadokhumenti asekelayo** — i-SHA-256 yezicelo, ukuhlolwa,
   izinkontileka ezisayindiwe, ama-oda okushintsha, izicelo zokukhokhelwa,
   amafomu okwamukelwa kokuhlinzekwa, izingeniso ze-ledger ye-treasury,
   amalogi okusayinda kwamalungelo. Ama-hash kuphela, hhayi okuqukethwe — ukuze
   kuvikelwe i-PII yokuhweba neyomuntu ngenkathi kunika amandla
   ukuqinisekiseka kwe-re-hash.
6. **Isimo se-audit nokweluleka** — yiliphi ibhizinisi lomhloli ngabukhulu
   elinegunya, yiliphi inhlangano enkulu yokuhlola, yiliphi ibhizinisi
   lokushushiswa elinegunya lokuthela inkohlakalo, izinsuku zokugcina
   ze-audit, isimo samanje sanoma yikuphi ukubuyekezwa okuqhubekayo.
7. **Ukuvulwa okuhambisanayo nerekhodi lokwala** — izimbangela zokuthenga
   ezilindile, izinhlolokhono zangaphakathi ezilindile, izicelo ze-FOI
   ezingakaphendulwa, ukubuyekezwa okuphikisayo, ukweqile okwaziwayo
   kwemigqa yebhajethi; uhlu olucacile lwama-pattern ithulkithi
   eyenqabe ukuwavala.
8. **Umphathi osayinayo** — uphathi wezezimali oqondene + indima +
   ukuphendulekisa komthetho + abasayindisi abahambisanayo ngaphansi
   kokuphathwa ngokuphindwe; yini ngamunye wabasayindi anikeza ubufakazi
   futhi yiniqu engahlinzeki ngabo siqu.

Iqiniso ngalinye liphetha ngenkambiso ye-refusal-gate: *"Leli qiniso libopha
kuphela u-X. Alibophi u-Y"* (lapho u-Y eyindlela yokwehluleka eseduze
kusukela esigabeni esithi "OKUNGAYENZAYO").

## Yini ithulkithi eyivikelayo

- **Ukushintshwa okuthuleyo kwemali yenkontileka ngemva kokukhishwa.** Noma yiluphi
  ushintsho kunoma yiliphi inani ku-FACT 04 lukhiqiza i-SHA-256
  ehlukile, impande yeMerkle ehlukile, futhi ihluleka ekuqinisekisweni
  kwe-OTS.
- **Ukushintshwa okuthuleyo komthengisi.** Ukubuyisela "Umthengisi A"
  ngo "Umthengisi B" enkontilekeni esevunyelwe kuyatholakala kuma-bytes
  avaliwe.
- **Ukubuyiselwa kwesikhathi sama-oda okushintsha.** I-FACT 04 ibambelela
  idethi yendoda yokushintsha kanye nesizathu ngomzuzu wokukhishwa;
  noma yisiphi isimangalo sakamuva sokuthi "lokhu kwakulokhu kungumkhakha
  ovumelwene" kufanele kubhekane nerekhodi le-oda lokushintsha elivaliwe.
- **Ukunyamalala okuzothile kwezinto ezingenanjabulo.** Konke
  okuthengiselwana okukhishwe esikhathini kuku-FACT 04 ngaphansi
  kwempande eyodwa yeMerkle. Ukushicilela iqoqo elincane kuyatholakala
  kuhlu lwama-hash amaqabunga.
- **Ukucindezelwa kokuvulwa okuhambisanayo.** I-FACT 07 ibambelela
  isimo sezimbangela ezilindile, izinhlolokhono ezilindile, kanye
  ne-FOI engakaphendulwa ngomzuzu wobufakazi. Noma yisiphi isimangalo
  sakamuva esithi "asikaze sizwe ngalokho kuphikiswa" siyacishwa
  komphakathi.
- **Ukushinta kwendawo yegunya le-audit.** I-FACT 06 ibambelela ukuthi
  yiziphi izinhlangano ezinendawo yokweluleka phezu kokuthengiselwana
  okuvaliwe. Isimangalo sakamuva sokuthi i-IG ethile "ayinalo igunya"
  kufanele sibhekane nerekhodi lendawo evaliwe.
- **Ukususwa kwerekhodi lokwala.** I-FACT 07 irekhoda lokho ithulkithi
  eyenqabile ukuvala; noma yisiphi isimangalo sakamuva esithi
  "asikaze senqabe lutho" siphikisana nerekhodi elivaliwe.

## Amacala omlando ithulkithi eyayilizosiza

Ithulkithi yayingakhipha **i-foreni labanikazi olusebenzayo** — *hhayi ukulungiswa,
hhayi ukuvinjelwa, hhayi indlela yokufakelana nokushushiswa kobuso bobugebengu* — emacaleni
arekhodelwe omphakathi abandakanya ukushintshwa okuthuleyo kwemibhalo
yokusebenzisa imali yomphakathi:

- **Brazil — *Operação Lava Jato* / umugqa wePetrobras (2014–2021).**
  Abashushisi befederal baseBrazil bafakaze isigungu sezinkampani ezikhulu
  zokwakha eziyokuthi iminyaka eyishumi zikhokha izimali zokufihla zilinganiselwa
  ku-~3% kuPetrobras nezinye izinkontileka zezakhiwo zefederal,
  nezimali zokufihla zihambisa kosopolitiki kanye nokulinganiselwa
  kwentengo kwesigungu ngokwaso. Izinkontileka njengoba zikhishwe
  zabonakala zijwayelekile; inkohlakalo yayingaphezulu kokukhishwa. Ithulkithi
  yokuvala AYIYIKHO ukuyivikela i-Lava Jato (izimali zokufihla zazifakwe
  entengweni NGAPHAMBI kokuba inkontileka ifike ekuvalweni), kepha yayizobophezela
  imigomo yenkontileka ngomzuzu wokukhishwa — ukwenza ukubuyisela kabusha
  kwe-foreni yakamuva kwe-"yini ngayinye yenkontileka eyathi kanjani
  ngempela ngenkathi isayinda" kubize kakhulu kakhulu. Izindleko zokuhlola
  ze-Lava Jato yayingxenye enkulu yokubiza ukuhlanganiswa kwesikhathi
  kusuka emarekhodini abekiwe angafanani.

- **European Union — izimpikiswano zezimali zokuthuthukiswa kwesifunda
  (amazwe amaningi alawayo, iminyaka yawo-2010–2020).** Imibiko yonyaka
  yeKomiti laseYurophu Lokuhlola yakhumbula amazinga aphelele wamaphutha
  kuzimali ze-European Structural and Investment Fund (ESIF) kanye
  ne-Cohesion Fund kusebenzisa imali, kweminye iminyaka kudlula umkhawulo
  wezimali. Izimpikiswano mayelana "nokuthi yini eyathiwa ngempela" ngokumelene
  "nokuthi yini eyalethwa ngempela" zibuya ngenxa yokuthi amadokhumenti
  okuthi angabuyekezwa phakathi kokubeka nokuhlolwa. Ukuvalwa kwe-MYRIAM
  ngokukhishwa kwesimangalo njengoba sibekiwe kwakuyobophezela okuqukethwe
  kwesimangalo ngasinye ngokubekwa, kuhlukanisa "lokho umthole othe
  kwesikhathi" kuya "kulokho ukubuyiselwa kabusha ngemva kokuhlolwa kuthe."

- **Iraq — Coalition Provisional Authority (CPA) ukukhokhwa kwemali,
  2003–2004.** I-CPA yakhokha cishe u-USD 9 billion ngokheshi kusukela
  ku-Development Fund for Iraq nesigunyaza esabhalwa ngu-Special Inspector
  General for Iraq Reconstruction (SIGIR) nemibiko yokuhlola yakamuva
  njengangaphelele. Izimpikiswano mayelana namarekhodi okukhokha
  ziqhubeka amashumi amabili eminyaka kamuva. Ukuvalwa ngomzuzu wokukhokha
  bekungeke kudale amarekhodi i-CPA engawagcinanga — kepha lapho noma
  yiliphi irekhodi laliphi (amabhuku abhalwe ngesandla, izimvume zokudlulisa
  ezinedethi), ukuvalwa kwesikhathi esifanayo bekuyobophezela isimo
  somzuzu wamabhayithi alelo rekhodi ngomzuzu wokukhishwa, kuhlukanisa
  "irekhodi ngesikhathi" "kunerekhodi njengoba labuyiswa kabusha kamuva."

- **Greece — izicebiselo zezikweletu zikahulumeni nokubuyekezwa kokubika
  kokusebenzisa imali yomphakathi (2009–2010).** Ukuhlolwa kwe-Eurostat
  ngo-2009–2010 kwakhumbula ukubuyekezwa okukhulu kwedatha yezezimali
  yaseGreece eyabikwa ngaphambi, kanye nendlela yokubuyekeza ngokwayo
  iphikiswa. Ukuvalwa kwemibiko yokusebenzisa imali yamakwati ngomzuzu
  wokushicilela kokuqala kwakuyokwenza i-*delta* phakathi kwemibiko yokuqala
  neyabuyiswa ibe ngavinjwa komphakathi esikhundleni sokwakhiwa kabusha
  ngokuqhathaniswa kwearchive kuphela. Ukuvalwa bekungeke kuvikele
  impikiswano yendlela yokulinganisa, kepha bekuyobophezela ikwati ngalinye
  elibikwe ngomzuzu wombiko wokuqala.

- **United Kingdom — ukuthenga kwesifo (2020–2022).** Imibiko ye-UK
  National Audit Office kanye namakomidi ephalamende yakhumbula izinqubo
  zokuthenga phakathi ne-2020–2021 lapho izinkontileka zazinikezwa ngaphansi
  kwezinhlinzeko zesimo esiphuthumayo nokuncipha kokuncintisana
  kanye nokuncipha kwamadokhumenti; ezinye iziphakamiso kamuva zazishintshwa,
  zikhansela, noma zikhokhwa. Ukuvalwa kwesiphakamiso ngasinye sokuthenga
  okuphuthumayo ngokukhishwa kwakuyobophezela isizathu sesiphakamiso nemigomo
  ngomzuzu wesiphakamiso, ukwenza izinguquko zakamuva zibe yi-delta evinjwe
  komphakathi esikhundleni sokuba yirekhodi elishintshwe ngokuthulile.

- **South Africa — izinkontileka zamabhizinisi omphakathi esikhathini
  sokuthumba umbuso (~2010–2018).** I-Zondo Commission yakhumbula
  izinkontileka kumabhizinisi amaningi okuhleliwe ombuso (Eskom, Transnet,
  Denel) amadokhumenti awo angaphelele, abuyekezwe, noma aphikiswa
  ngendlela ehlukile. Ukuvalwa kwenkontileka ngokunikezwa ngomzuzu
  wokukhishwa kwakuyobophezela isimo serekhodi njengoba sinikezwe,
  ukuhlukanisa imiphumela yokwakhiwa kabusha ye-foreni yakamuva
  kunerekhodi lesikhathi esifanayo.

Ithulkithi YAYINGEKE iyivikele inkohlakalo eseyisisekelo kunoma yiliphi
lawa macala angenhla. Emacaleni amaningana inkinga eseyisisekelo yayikuthi
inkohlakalo yayi-*ingaphezulu kokukhishwa* — ukuvalwa kubophezela inkontileka
enenkohlakalo ngobuqotho obukhulu, okuyinto ehlukile yokusiza (i-foreni labanikazi
yokuphendulekisa kamuva) kodwa engefani nokuvinjelwa. Kwamanye amacala
(CPA cash, ukusebenzisa imali okungaphandle kwebhajethi) inkinga eseyisisekelo
yayingekho noma yayingagcinwanga amarekhodi ayevalwa — ithulkithi
ayikwazi ukudala amarekhodi igunya elingawakhiqizanga.

Lawa macala omlando akuqanjwe ngokuhambisana nemiphumela ekhishwe komphakathi
(amarekhodi enkantolo, imibiko yamakomidi ephalamende, ukushicilelwa
kwezinhlangano zokuhlola). Akukho simangalo esenziwa mayelana nomuntu ongumuntu;
imiphumela yezinhlangano ebhalwe ime erekhodini lomphakathi.

## Nini ungasebenzisa le thulkithi

- Indawo yakho yokuphathwa inenkambiso esisekelwe yokubika ukwenza
  ibhajethi futhi ifuna ukufaka ibhanga lomphakathi elingaguquki.
- Ulindele ukuhlolwa kwesimo ngemva kokukhishwa (izicelo ze-FOI,
  ukuhlolwa, ukuqashwa komphakathi, ukuphenya kombiki wezindaba) futhi ufuna
  iphuzu lokubhekisa elibonakalisa ukushintshwa.
- Unelungelo lomthetho ngaphansi komthetho wokuvuleka / wamarekhodi avulekile
  ukushicilela isethi yamaqiniso elohlu, ngokuhlonipha ukukhutshwa
  kwesizathu / sokuphepha.
- Usebenza ngaphansi komlando wokuhlolwa kahulumeni (umhloli ngabukhulu,
  inhlangano enkulu yokuhlola) futhi ufuna ukubophezela ukuvalwa
  kwekwati ngalinye ngaphambi komjikelo wokuhlolwa ukuthi ufike kuwo.
- Ushicilela ngaphansi kwe-Open Contracting Data Standard noma i-IATI futhi
  ufuna ukufakwa okukhonkretha okuhambisanayo nokushicilelwa kwekhasi.

## Nini UNGAYISEBENZISI le thulkithi

- **Ungayisebenzisi njengokuthatha indawo yokuhlolwa komthetho, ukubuyekezwa
  ngumhloli ngabukhulu, ukuphenya kokushushisa, noma ukubukwa
  komphakathi.** Ithulkithi *iyengeza*, hhayi ukubuyisela.
- **Ungayisebenzisi njengesitifiketi sobuqotho.** Irekhodi lokusebenzisa imali
  elivaliwe akusiyo isitifiketi sokukhululeka enkohlakalweni. Ukubona
  njengayo yikho ukuhluleka ithulkithi yokwakhiwa kokuqala kwemiqala
  ekhona ukuze ivikele.
- **Ungayisebenzisi phakathi nesikhathi.** Ithulkithi yenzelwe umzuzu
  wokukhishwa okusemthethweni (ukuvalwa kwakamuva, ukunikezwa kwenkontileka,
  ukukhokhwa kwesibekiso). Ukuvalwa phakathi nesikhathi kudala amaphuzu
  okubhekisa adukisayo.
- **Ungayisebenzisi ukushilo ukuthi ukusebenzisa imali kwakusemthethweni,
  kufanele, noma kukhululekile enkohlakalweni** — ithulkithi ayikwazi ukukwenza lokho.
- **Ungayisebenzisi ukuvala ukusebenzisa imali okuhlanzwe, i-PII yomsebenzi
  ongumuntu ngamunye, noma i-PII yomthengisi yokuhweba.** Ithulkithi yenqaba
  lezi zinhlobo; ukuzama kudala irekhodi lokwala (i-FACT 07).
- **Ungayisebenzisi ukuvala ukulungiswa kwangaphambi kokukhishwa.** Ukulungiswa
  kungavalwa ngaphansi kohlobo lobufakazi olwehlukile ("ukulungiswa kwephuzu, akukhishwa"),
  kodwa hhayi ngaphansi kohlobo lokukhishwa lwale thulkithi.
- **Ungayisebenzisi ngaphansi kwengcindezi yokushiya ukuvulwa kwe-FACT 07.**
  Yenqabe, futhi uvale ukwala.
- **Ungayisebenzisi uma igunya lakho lezezimali libanjiwe.** Ukusebenzisa imali
  okukhwabanisayo okuvaliwe kuyirekhodi lomphakathi elingaguquki lokukhwabanisa,
  hhayi ukuvikelwa kwakho. Iprimitive isika izindlela zombili; lokho
  yiqiniso layo.


**Isikhathi esibalulekile:** vala KU isikhathi sokukhishwa okusemthethweni — okusho,
umzuzu lapho umphathi wezezimali oqondene asayinda ukuvalwa kwakwati,
umzuzu lapho inkontileka inikezwa ngokusemthethweni, noma umzuzu lapho
ukukhokhwa kukhishwe ngokusemthethweni. Ukuvalwa ngaphambi kokukhishwa okusemthethweni
kudala iphuzu lokubhekisa elingelona-iqiniso "lokhu kuyirekhodi elisemthethweni."
Ukuvalwa esikhathini eside ngemva kokukhishwa okusemthethweni kuyizothando
ngokomzimba ngokumelene noshintsho lwesikhathi.

**Isigaba:** ithulkithi ihlelwe yobufakazi bekwati njengezimemezelo. Izigaba
ezingaphansi kwekwati (ukuvalwa kwenyanga, ukuvalwa kwe-transaction) kuyasekelwa
ngokuphinde uqalise nokumboza okufanele; ukuvalwa kwe-transaction esodwa
yenani eliphezulu kuyisetshenziswa olusemthethweni.

## Ukufakwa nengqalasizinda yezezimali zomphakathi ekhona

Ithulkithi yenzelwe ukuhlanganisa, hhayi ukubuyisela:

- **Open Contracting Data Standard (OCDS) v1.1.5.** Amaphuzu amahlanu okushicilela
  (ukuhlela, isiphakamiso, ukunikezwa, inkontileka, ukufezekisa) ahambisana
  ngokwemvelo no-FACT 03 (ukuhlela → inkokhelo), FACT 04 (isiphakamiso,
  ukunikezwa, inkontileka, ukufezekisa → manifesti yokuthengiselwana),
  kanye no-FACT 05 (ama-hash amadokhumenti asekelayo). Ukuthunyelwa kwe-OCDS JSON
  ngokwakho kuvalwa ku-FACT 05 (amadokhumenti okulawula). Isizinda
  esibalulekile esisebenzisa ukushicilela kwe-OCDS singafaka ukuvalwa
  kwe-MYRIAM njengelungele lekhonkretha phezu kwekhasi laso elikhona.

- **International Aid Transparency Initiative (IATI) standard v2.03.**
  Ngokusebenzisa imali okuhlinzekwe ngokuphelele noma okwengxenye yokusiza
  okungaphandle, i-IATI XML ingavalwa njengedokhumenti yokulawula;
  ukuvalwa kuhlinzeka inhlanganiselo yekhonkretha eyimpilekhasi yokushicilela
  ye-HTTP ye-IATI Registry.

- **Open Government Partnership (OGP) izinkambiso.** Izinhlelo zezizwe ze-OGP
  ngokuvamile zifaka izinkambiso zokuvuleka kwezezimali; ukuvalwa
  kwe-MYRIAM kufaka isazinkambiso "sokushicilela okubonakalisa ukushintshwa"
  ngendlela engaqinisekiseka ngokuzimele yinhlangano yomphakathi ngaphandle
  kokudinga ukwethemba kuwebusayithi yikahulumeni.

- **OECD Recommendation on Public Integrity (2017).** Ithulkithi
  isekela Recommendation 4 (idatha yokuvulekile yikahulumeni kuzezimali
  zomphakathi ngohlobo olufundekayo ngumshini, ngesikhathi sokushicilela)
  ngokwenza idatha ibe namabhanga ekhonkretha kanye nokufundeka ngumshini.

- **UN Convention against Corruption (UNCAC), ikakhulukazi i-Article 9
  (ukuthenga) kanye no-13 (ukubamba iqhaza komphakathi).** Ithulkithi
  ihlinzeka iprimitive ye-foreni labanikazi esekela izibopho zokuvuleka
  ze-Article 9 nokubukwa komphakathi ku-Article 13.

- **Supreme Audit Institutions (SAIs) — uhlaka lwe-INTOSAI.** Ama-SAI
  asebenza ngaphansi kwemikhakha ye-INTOSAI (ISSAI) angasebenzisa
  okushicilelwe okuvaliwe njengobufakazi ngobuqotho obukhulu; ama-hash
  e-SHA-256 ku-FACT 05 ayilawo ama-hash amathuluzi e-audit-e ye-SAI
  angakwazi ukuwabuyekeza.

- **Amahhovisi omhloli ngabukhulu.** Amahhovisi e-IG asekhaya
  anendawo yokuphatha ngokusebenzisa imali okuvaliwe (FACT 06)
  angabuyekeza izindiya zamadokhumenti ama-hash awo avaliwe ku-FACT 05;
  ukuvalwa kubophezela imizimba yedokhumenti etholwe ngaphansi
  kwemibuso kuya emizimbeni ebikhona ngokukhishwa.

- **Imithetho yokukhululekile yolwazi kanye namarekhodi avulekile.**
  Ukuvalwa akukhululi igunya elikhipha kuzibopho zokuqhubekayo ze-FOI;
  empeleni, kwenza impendulo ye-FOI ekhethiwe itholakale njengokuphambana
  kwerekhodi elivaliwe. I-FACT 07 C3 ibophezela izicelo ze-FOI ezilindile
  ngesikhathi sobufakazi.

- **Amaregistry omnikazi obuhle (FATF Recommendation 24, EU AML
  directives, US Corporate Transparency Act).** Idatha yomnikazi
  obuhle AYIVALI ngaphansi kwale thulkithi (i-FACT 04 isebenzisa ubunikazi
  bomthengisi kusukela egameni lebhizinisi lomthetho elirejistriwe
  kuphela). Ukuvulwa komnikazi obuhle yiphrothokholi yokuvala
  ehlukile engakhipha ukushicilela okufanayo okuhlukana okubhekiselwa
  ngempande yeMerkle.

- **Ukuvuleka okuphethwe enkantolo (ukubika kokusebenzisa imali komthethonkantolo,
  izinhlelo zokubuyisa impahla).** Imiphumela yezinhlelo zenkantolo
  zokuthi inkohlakalo ayingaphansi kwendawo yale thulkithi kodwa
  ingavalwa ngaphansi kwezinhlobo zobufakazi ezifanayo.

## Yini ithulkithi engayibuyiseli

- Ukuhlolwa kwemithetho yokwenza ibhajethi ngamabhizinisi omhloli ngabukhulu
- Ukubuyekezwa kwe-supreme-audit-institution (GAO, NAO, TCU, CAG, AGN, BRH,
  Cour des comptes, okulinganayo)
- Ukuphenywa kwabashushisi kwecala lenkohlakalo (abashushisi besizwe
  abalwa nenkohlakalo, izinhlangano ze-UNCAC Article 36)
- Ukubika kombiki wezindaba kanye nokuphenywa komphakathi
- Izigodi zababiki nemikhakha yokuvulwa okuvikelwe
- Ukuvulwa komnikazi obuhle
- Ukubuyekezwa kwabaseduze kwamazwe omhlaba (OECD, IMF Fiscal Transparency
  Evaluation, EU semester, IATI registry)
- Ukubuyiselwa kwempahla okuphethwe enkantolo

Ukuvalwa kuyiprimitive ye-foreni labanikazi ehlisa izindleko futhi yandisa
ukuthembeka kwakho konke okungenhla. Akusiyo, futhi ngeke ibe, ukubuyisela
kunoma yini yazo.
