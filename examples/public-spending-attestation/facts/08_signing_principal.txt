Public officials in named public-office roles ARE properly identified
in this fact (it is the load-bearing accountability hook of the seal),

capacities is NOT.

This fact asserts only the signing-principal identification fields for
the fictional attestation as declared. It does not assert any of those
fields about any real public official, finance authority, or
jurisdiction. The criminal-misstatement provisions referenced apply
ONLY in a real attestation by a real official under real statutory
authority; this fictional example imposes no such accountability on any
real person.
