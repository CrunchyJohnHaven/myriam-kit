VENDOR IDENTIFICATION POLICY (READ FIRST)

  This fact identifies vendors by REGISTERED LEGAL ENTITY NAME and
  jurisdiction-issued business identifier ONLY. It does NOT identify:
    - Individual employees, officers, or beneficial owners
    - Personal phone numbers, email addresses, or home addresses
    - Bank account numbers, tax IDs of natural persons, or other PII

  The vendor entity name is what is on the publicly registered contract
  and what appears in the jurisdiction's procurement portal. Beneficial-
  ownership disclosure is a SEPARATE oversight regime (Fictional
  Beneficial Ownership Registry §601) whose output may be sealed in a
  parallel publication but is NOT included here. The seal of beneficial
  ownership requires its own legal and privacy review.

beneficial owners; that pricing was at fair market value; or that any
underlying transaction was free of corruption, collusion, or kickbacks.
The seal anchors WHAT WAS REPORTED. Investigation of WHAT WAS TRUE is
the jurisdiction of the audit, inspector-general, and judicial systems.
