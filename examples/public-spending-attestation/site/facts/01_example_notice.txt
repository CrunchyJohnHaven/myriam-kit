FACT 01 — EXAMPLE NOTICE

This publication is an EXAMPLE demonstration of how a municipal, state,
provincial, or national government finance authority can use myriam-kit
to seal a public-spending attestation at the moment of budget execution.
The jurisdiction, statutory references, appropriation lines, contract
identifiers, vendor entity names, disbursement amounts, audit bodies,
and signing officials are ALL FICTIONAL.

The purpose: show the SHAPE of a real public-spending attestation seal.
A real attestation would replace each fact body with the actual fields
of an actual budget-execution event (contract award, milestone
disbursement, deliverable acceptance) as it is issued by the responsible
finance authority.

This kit is intended to compose with — not replace — existing public-
finance infrastructure (statutory appropriation processes, treasury
disbursement systems, supreme audit institutions, inspector-general
offices, freedom-of-information regimes, the Open Contracting Data
Standard, the International Aid Transparency Initiative, and equivalent
domestic transparency portals). The MYRIAM seal provides Bitcoin-
anchored tamper-evidence on top of whatever publication regime the
jurisdiction already operates.

The seal anchors WHAT WAS REPORTED AT ISSUANCE. It does not anchor
whether the underlying transaction was lawful, free of corruption,
priced at market rate, or delivered as specified. Those are downstream
questions for auditors, inspectors-general, courts, and journalists.
The seal makes the as-reported state of the record permanent and
publicly diffable against later versions.

This fact asserts only that the publication is an example, not a real
public-spending attestation. No real expenditure is hereby attested.
No real official, vendor, or jurisdiction is hereby named.
