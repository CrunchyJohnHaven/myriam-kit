  - International Aid Transparency Initiative (IATI):
    Not applicable to this publication (no international aid).
  - Donor/co-funder reporting:
    National co-funded transactions trigger separate quarterly reports
    to the relevant national ministries; this seal is in addition to
    those reports, not in lieu of them.
  - Freedom-of-information / public-records statutes:
    The seal does not exempt the municipality from continuing FOI
    response obligations; in fact, the seal makes selective FOI
    response detectable as a deviation from the sealed record.

This fact asserts only the audit and oversight context as it existed
at the moment of attestation. It does not assert that any audit, IG
review, or SAI examination has been completed, that any completed
audit has found expenditures lawful, or that the named oversight
bodies have the resources to exercise their jurisdiction in fact.
The seal records who SHOULD review; whether they DO review, and what
they FIND, is a downstream and entirely separate question.
