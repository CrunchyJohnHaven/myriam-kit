This fact asserts only the companion disclosures above as
recorded for the fictional decision. It does NOT assert:
  - that the disclosed prior decisions, pending appeals, or
    recusals are exhaustive of all such facts that exist
    (omission is itself a sealable fact if discovered later),
  - that the AI system disclosure exhausts all algorithmic
    contributions to the decisionmaking pipeline (the seal
    relies on the carrier's good-faith disclosure;
    undisclosed algorithmic systems remain undetectable by
    the seal alone),
  - that the override-rate disclosure (if included) is
    statistically meaningful at the per-reviewer level (the
    aggregate metric across many seals over time is more
    meaningful than any single seal's number).
