clinical criteria were applied by the carrier's licensed UR
reviewer as the basis for the decision in FACT 05. The exact
guideline source, version, edition, and revision date are
captured because silent retroactive criteria revision —
specifically the pattern of a carrier asserting after the
fact that "we used the updated criteria" when in fact the
original decision was made against a different version — is
one of the load-bearing failure modes this seal is designed
to make publicly detectable.

  CRITERIA-VERSION NON-REVISION COMMITMENT

The certifying licensed signing principal (FACT 08) attests that:

  - The criteria-source, edition, version year, quarterly
    update, revision date, subsection, and subsections-applied
    list above are the EXACT criteria that were applied to this
    case at the moment of the decision in FACT 05.
  - No subsequent revision of the cited criteria has been
    silently applied to this decision. If the publisher
    (Milliman/InterQual/carrier) issues a revision of the cited
    criteria after the date of this seal, the revision applies
    only to NEW decisions rendered against the revised version
    and does NOT retroactively become the basis for this
    decision.
  - If this decision is subsequently amended (e.g. on appeal,
    on external review, on regulator demand), the amendment is
    a NEW myriam-kit publication that references this one by
    Merkle root, with FACT 03 of the amendment disclosing the
    criteria applied to the amended decision (which may be a
    different version than the original).

This fact asserts only the criteria-source citation, the
subsection-applied enumeration, the secondary-policy citation,
the category-level diagnosis disclosure, and the non-revision
commitment as recorded for the fictional decision. It does
NOT assert:
  - that the cited criteria are medically correct,
  - that the cited criteria reflect current standard of care,
  - that the cited criteria were appropriate for this
    specific patient's clinical presentation,
  - that the criteria-development process was unbiased,
  - that the carrier substantively applied the cited criteria
    (the seal records what was CITED; detecting a gap between
    cited and actually-applied criteria requires audit of the
    decisionmaking record, which is upstream of the seal per
    the README "DOES NOT do" section item 8).
