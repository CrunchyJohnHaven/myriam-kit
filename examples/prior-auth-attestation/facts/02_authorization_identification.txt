This fact binds the sealed publication to a named, regulated
carrier acting under a specific, regulated plan type and to a
named, NPPES-registered provider organization, requesting on
behalf of a privacy-protected member identifier handle, for a
category-level service category. It does NOT name the member,
the member's family, the specific diagnosis (see FACT 03 for
criteria-match disclosure constraints), the specific CPT/HCPCS
code where re-identification risk applies, or any other private
individual by legal name beyond the requesting clinician (whose
individual NPI is published because it is the operative requester
and already public). The carrier-and-plan citation is included so
the seal can be located within the carrier's UM-system case file
and the state insurance department's UM-program filing by any
party with standing.

This fact asserts only the authorization-identification, plan
citation, provider organization citation, member-handle binding,
service-category framing, and temporal context for the fictional
authorization. It does not assert:
  - that the plan was in force on the service date(s)
    requested (that is a coverage question — see FACTS 03 and 05),
  - that the requesting clinician's credentialing was current
    with the carrier (a separate credentialing question),
  - that the member is eligible for the specific service
    requested under the plan's benefit design,
  - that the prior-auth review was substantively correct (see
    FACT 05 for the decision and the limits on what the seal
    asserts about substantive correctness),
  - that the carrier owes coverage for the service.
