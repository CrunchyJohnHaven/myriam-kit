FACT 07 — COMPANION DISCLOSURES AND REFUSAL RECORD (FICTIONAL)

Per the MYRIAM kit's "constraint-first" design principle, this fact
records what the kit explicitly refused to seal and any companion
disclosures that the certifying authority is obliged to publish along
with the tally so that selective omission is detectable.

COMPANION DISCLOSURES (sealed alongside the tally so the tally cannot
be read in isolation)

  C1. PENDING LITIGATION
      No pending pre-certification litigation against this election at
      certification moment. (FICTIONAL)

  C2. POST-COUNT ANOMALIES UNDER REVIEW
      None at certification moment. (FICTIONAL — a real attestation
      would name specific anomalies, e.g. precinct X with unusual
      under-vote pattern, ballot drop-box Y with chain-of-custody
      irregularity.)

  C3. EXCLUDED COHORTS
      No cohorts excluded from the certified count beyond those
      enumerated in FACT 04 (provisional rejections, mail-ballot
      rejections, late UOCAVA). No "set-aside" cohorts.

  C4. DISSENTING BOARD MEMBERS
      The certification vote of the Board of Canvassers (FACT 02) was
      3-0 unanimous. No dissenting opinions filed. (FICTIONAL — a real
      attestation would name dissenters and seal their dissent text
      hash here, NOT to silence dissent but to make it part of the
      permanent public record.)

REFUSAL RECORD — patterns the kit refused to seal

The kit does NOT seal the following types of facts, even if requested
by the certifying authority:

  R1. Individual voter identification or voting choices
  R2. Per-ballot signature-match decisions (PII)
  R3. Precinct-level results for precincts where N < 50 ballots
      (small-cell PII risk)
  R4. Tallies before all ballots are counted (partial-count seals
      create false certainty)
  R5. Provisional or conditional certifications ("if X then Y")
  R6. Sealing at request of any party other than the official
      certifying authority for this jurisdiction
  R7. Counts that exclude pending-litigation cohorts without explicit
      disclosure in C3 above

If during certification any request matching R1-R7 was made and
refused, that refusal would be documented as a sealed fact:
  "Refusal #N: party X requested sealing of [pattern]; the kit
  refused per refusal-rule R[m]."

  Refusals this certification: NONE. (FICTIONAL)

This fact asserts only the companion-disclosure status and any
refusal record at the moment of certification. It does not assert that
the refusal-rule list is exhaustive of all PII patterns or that all
companion disclosures have been identified by the certifying authority.
