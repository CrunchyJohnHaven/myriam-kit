FACT 01 — EXAMPLE NOTICE

This publication is an EXAMPLE demonstration of how a third-party social /
labor / environmental auditor, an in-house supplier-compliance team, or an
NGO running a supply-chain investigation can use myriam-kit to seal an
audit-report attestation at the moment of sign-off. The supplier identifier,
audit body, auditor names, scope, findings, dates, and all numerical and
qualitative parameters are FICTIONAL.

The purpose: show the SHAPE of a real supply-chain audit-report seal. A
real attestation would replace each fact body with the actual elements of
the actual audit.

This kit is intended to compose with — not replace — existing supply-chain
audit infrastructure (SA8000, RBA VAP, amfori BSCI, SMETA, FSC chain-of-
custody, ISO 26000 guidance, B Corp impact assessment, Fair Labor
Association, Higg Index). The MYRIAM seal provides Bitcoin-anchored
tamper-evidence on top of whatever certification regime the auditor
already operates under.

CRITICAL HONESTY: the seal anchors the audit report as the auditor issued
it. The seal does NOT and CANNOT verify that the audit findings are
correct. Supply-chain audits have a documented history of being captured
by industry, of missing severe abuses immediately before they surface as
public disasters (e.g. Rana Plaza was audited months before collapse),
and of relying on announced visits and translator-mediated interviews
that can be coached. Sealing a captured audit creates a permanent
public record of the captured audit. That is its honesty, not its
defense.

This fact asserts only that the publication is an example, not a real
audit report. No real supplier, factory, worker, auditor, brand, or
certification body is hereby named or attested.
