QUALIFICATION HONESTY

  - "Qualified" means the auditors hold credentials issued by training
    bodies whose curricula are themselves of contested adequacy. A
    credentialed auditor can still miss severe abuses (Rana Plaza was
    audited by credentialed auditors). The seal anchors credentials,
    not competence.
  - "Independent" under standard industry definitions does NOT mean
    "free of structural pressure to soften findings." Brand-paid audit
    bodies face a documented pipeline-incentive to deliver findings
    that retain commissioning-brand relationships. This kit seals the
    disclosure; the seal does not solve the conflict.

This fact asserts only the qualifications and independence declarations
as recorded for the fictional audit. It does not assert that the
declarations are exhaustive of structural conflicts of interest, that
the audit body's accreditation entails detection of all violations the
standard targets, or that brand-commissioned audits are an adequate
governance model for the harms in scope.
