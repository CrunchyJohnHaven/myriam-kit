manifest of findings against the audit standard (FACT 02), as those
findings stood at the moment of report sign-off (FACT 02 audit report date).

Each finding is referenced by category, severity, and the SHA-256 of the
full finding write-up in the auditor's report. The raw write-ups are NOT
included in the public publication (worker quotes, room photographs,
document scans, and translator notes can re-identify individuals); only

A regulator, brand, NGO, worker representative, court, or downstream
investigator can request the full finding write-up for any row above.
Re-hashing the received write-up with SHA-256 and comparing to the row
above verifies that the document received is the document that existed
at sign-off. Any post-sign-off softening, deletion, or recategorization
of any finding produces a different hash and is publicly detectable.

CRITICAL HONESTY ON FINDINGS:

  - This manifest is the audit-as-issued, NOT the audit-as-correct.
  - Audits at semi-announced facilities have documented blind spots:
    (a) coached workers, (b) prepared documents, (c) hidden dormitories
    or production lines, (d) temporary removal of underage workers,
    (e) hidden subcontracting to non-audited facilities.
  - The absence of CRITICAL_NC findings does NOT prove absence of
    critical violations. It proves only that the audit team did not
    identify any in the time, language, and access conditions described
    in FACT 04.
  - The presence of a CONFORMANT row does NOT prove the element is
    actually being honored — it asserts only that the auditors did
    not find evidence to the contrary within their methodology.

This fact asserts only the audit findings as recorded by the auditors
at sign-off for the fictional audit. It does not assert that the
findings are accurate, exhaustive, or free of capture; it does not
assert that closing the listed non-conformances entails substantive
improvement in worker conditions; and it does not assert that the
absence of a finding is evidence of conformance.
