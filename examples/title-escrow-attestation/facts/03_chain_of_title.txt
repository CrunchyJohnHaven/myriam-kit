    fiduciary capacity beyond what is recorded.

    reference in Idx 5 is recorded only as the index name; the
    underlying decree is not published here).

This fact asserts only the chain of recorded conveyances above,
as transcribed from the Fictional County Recorder's certified-
copy service at the search effective date specified in FACT 02.
It does not assert:
  - that any recorded conveyance was legally effective to
    transfer the title it purports to transfer,
  - that any grantor had the capacity (legal capacity, mental
    capacity, marital-property-act compliance) to convey,
  - that any signature is authentic (a fraudulently recorded
    deed is recorded honestly here as what the recorder showed),
  - that the chain establishes marketable title under the law of
    the situs state — marketability is a contract-law
    determination separate from this factual record,
  - that no off-record conveyance has occurred (off-record
    matters are excepted in FACT 06).
