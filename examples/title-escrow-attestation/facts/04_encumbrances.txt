This fact asserts only the recorded encumbrances above, as
they appeared in the Fictional County Recorder's index at the
search effective date specified in FACT 02. It does not
assert:
  - the priority order of the encumbrances under the law of
    the situs state (priority is a legal determination that
    depends on factors including recording-act type, knowledge,
    and federal-tax-lien override rules),
  - that any encumbrance is currently enforceable (some
    encumbrances may be barred by statutes of limitations,
    waived, satisfied without release-recording, or otherwise
    unenforceable),
  - that any encumbrance has been satisfied if no release is
    of record (a paid mortgage that has not been released
    appears here as still recorded; satisfaction is a
    contractual matter between borrower and lender that may
    require curative recording),
  - that no off-record encumbrance exists (off-record
    matters are excepted in FACT 06).
