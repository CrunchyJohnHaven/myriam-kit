FACT 08 — SIGNING PRINCIPAL (FICTIONAL)

The fictional signing principal of this example whistleblower disclosure:

  Name:               Jane Doe (FICTIONAL)
  Capacity:           Whistleblower; former Senior Quality Engineer
                      at Acme Aerospace (fictional company)
  Date sealed:        2026-05-21
  Cryptographic key:  no key in this example (a real disclosure could
                      attach a PGP public key fingerprint here, so
                      future statements claimed to be from the
                      whistleblower can be cryptographically verified;
                      omitted here because no real person exists)

In a real disclosure, the signing principal section should include:

  - The whistleblower's legal name OR a pseudonym they will
    consistently use across all related publications.
  - Their role/capacity at the time of acquiring the evidence.
  - A PGP public key OR equivalent (so future statements claimed to
    be from them can be cryptographically verified against this seal).
  - A canonical contact channel (typically an attorney, not the
    whistleblower's personal contact).
  - Date and time of sealing.

The whistleblower SHOULD NOT include in this fact:

  - Personal contact information (use an attorney).
  - Home address or other locating information.
  - Identifying information about family members.
  - The whistleblower's own identity if they are remaining anonymous;
    in that case the principal field is the pseudonym, and the
    whistleblower's attorney serves as the practical principal for
    communications.

This fact asserts only the signing principal identification fields for
the fictional whistleblower as declared. It does not assert any of
those fields about any real person.
