FACT 02 — PROVENANCE OF EVIDENCE (FICTIONAL)

The fictional Jane Doe was employed as a Senior Quality Engineer at
"Acme Aerospace" (fictional) from 2024-03-15 to 2026-04-01. During that
period she had authorized access to internal quality-assurance records
under her employment agreement.

The evidence referenced in FACT 03 was acquired in the following ways
(each item separately):

  - Quality reports she authored or co-authored: produced as part of
    her work, retained as part of her personal work records under her
    employment agreement's whistleblower-protection clause.
  - Email communications to and from her: forwarded to her personal
    archive in real time as received, per the email retention practice
    she had maintained since 2020 across all employers.
  - One internal slide deck: photographed during a meeting she
    attended, with her personal phone, on 2025-11-08.

No evidence in FACT 03 was acquired by unauthorized access to systems,
by impersonation of other employees, by circumvention of access
controls, or by exfiltrating documents she was not authorized to view.

This fact asserts only the provenance chain above for the fictional
evidence set. It does not assert that all whistleblowers' evidence is
acquired this cleanly, or that this provenance pattern is sufficient
for any specific legal defense.
