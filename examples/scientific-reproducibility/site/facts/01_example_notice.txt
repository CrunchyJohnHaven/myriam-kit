This publication is an EXAMPLE demonstration of how a research group
can use myriam-kit to seal a reproducibility manifest for a paper or
preprint. The paper, authors, dataset, analysis pipeline, and all
hashes are FICTIONAL.

The purpose: show the SHAPE of a real reproducibility seal. A research
group would replace each fact body with the actual artifacts of an
actual study.

This kit is intended to compose with existing scholarly infrastructure
(arXiv, bioRxiv, OSF, GitHub, Zenodo). The MYRIAM seal provides
Bitcoin-anchored, tamper-evident attestation that a specific snapshot
of (paper draft, raw data, analysis pipeline, expected result) existed
in a specific form at a specific time.

This fact asserts only that the publication is an example, not a real
reproducibility seal. No real paper is hereby published. No real
