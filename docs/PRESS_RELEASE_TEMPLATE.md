# Press Release Template: First Sealed Attestation

*Ready-to-use copy for any institution announcing their first
publicly sealed attestation. CC0. Fork and customize.*

The activation energy for an institution to do something novel for the
first time is much higher than to repeat what others have already done.
This template reduces the activation energy by providing pre-written
copy that any institution can adapt in 15 minutes.

## Why this matters for the institution

A sealed attestation produces a measurable transparency signal that:

1. **Differentiates the institution from peers** that have not yet
   committed.
2. **Is cited in funding applications**, accreditation reviews,
   compliance audits, and grant proposals.
3. **Reduces dispute exposure** by establishing what was attested at
   what moment.
4. **Costs roughly nothing** (~5 minutes per attestation, ~$0 in
   infrastructure).
5. **Is reversible at any time** if the institution decides the
   commitment is no longer appropriate (though the prior sealed
   attestations remain immutable).

There is no rational reason for an institution that does not intend
silent revision to refuse this. The first institution in each domain
will be celebrated. The hundredth will be expected.
