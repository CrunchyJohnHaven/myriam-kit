FACT 02 — SIGNING PRINCIPAL

[Replace this body with a declaration of who is sealing the publication
and under what authority.]

Example:

  This publication is sealed by [name or entity] in the capacity of
  [role / title / standing]. The signing principal accepts responsibility
  for the contents of all sealed facts in this publication.

  Communications about this publication may be directed to
  [contact channel — email, address, etc.]. The signing principal does
  not guarantee a response.

  This fact asserts only the identity of the signing principal and the
  channel for communications. It does not assert that the principal will
  respond to any particular message.
